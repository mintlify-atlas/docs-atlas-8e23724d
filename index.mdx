DataOps TestGen is DataKitchen's open-source data quality platform. It scans your databases, learns their characteristics through profiling, generates validation tests automatically, and continuously watches for anomalies in freshness, volume, schema drift, and custom metrics. You can operate TestGen entirely through its web UI, automate workflows via CLI, or connect AI agents through the built-in MCP server.

## How TestGen works

TestGen follows a four-step data quality workflow that takes you from raw database to continuous quality monitoring.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Profile your data">
    Point TestGen at a table group in your database. The profiler scans every column and computes statistics — value distributions, nullability, uniqueness, patterns, and more — building a detailed picture of your data's shape.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate tests automatically">
    Based on the profile, TestGen generates hundreds of tailored data quality validation tests. Tests cover completeness, consistency, validity, uniqueness, referential integrity, and statistical thresholds — no manual authoring required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run tests on every data refresh">
    Execute your test suite against each new data load. TestGen compares results against the generated thresholds and flags failures with detailed drill-downs showing exactly which rows and columns failed and why.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor continuously for anomalies">
    Set up monitors to track freshness, row volume, schema changes, and custom metrics over time. TestGen surfaces anomalies automatically and can send notifications when thresholds are breached.
  </Step>
</Steps>
